# Hypothesis Validation Survey (H1-H6)

## Survey design

- Target sample: mixed Admin/Judge/Entrant users
- Minimum responses: 100 overall, with >=25 per persona where possible
- Scale: 1-7 Likert unless stated
- Include screening and role-based branching

## Screening

1. Which role best describes your primary use?
- Admin
- Judge
- Entrant
- Other (exclude)

2. How recently did you use an awards/grants platform?
- Within 3 months
- 3-12 months
- 12-24 months
- More than 24 months (exclude)

---

## H1 (Admin): tile dashboard preference

- "I prefer a tile overview for monitoring program readiness over menu-first navigation."
- "A tile dashboard helps me identify blockers faster than sidebar navigation alone."

Behavioral check:
- "In your last program, how did you usually find setup blockers?"
  - Dashboard overview
  - Sidebar navigation
  - Export/spreadsheet
  - Team chat/email

Confirm H1 if:
- Mean >=5.2 on both attitudinal items and >=50% indicate faster blocker finding in overview-first flow.

Reject H1 if:
- Mean <4.5 and users rely mainly on direct menu paths for speed.

---

## H2 (Judge): split-panel preference

- "I score faster when submission content and scoring panel are visible side-by-side."
- "Switching between content and scoring screens breaks my concentration."

Behavioral check:
- "Preferred judging layout"
  - Split panel
  - Full-screen content then score
  - Criterion-by-criterion wizard

Confirm H2 if:
- Mean >=5.5 and split panel selected by >=60% judges.

Reject H2 if:
- Full-screen or wizard is preferred by majority or no productivity gain is reported.

---

## H3 (Entrant): abandonment reason

- "I stop submissions primarily when requirements are unclear."
- "Technical issues are the main reason I abandon submissions." (reverse signal)

Behavioral check:
- "Main reason you paused or abandoned last submission"
  - Unclear requirements
  - Time constraints
  - Technical problems
  - Team coordination issues
  - Other

Confirm H3 if:
- Unclear requirements is top reason and technical issues are secondary.

Reject H3 if:
- Technical issues is top reason in entrant segment.

---

## H4 (Judge): save confidence

- "A persistent saved indicator reduces my anxiety while scoring."
- "I have re-submitted scores because I was unsure they were saved."

Confirm H4 if:
- Mean >=5.5 for saved indicator value and >=40% report prior save uncertainty behaviors.

Reject H4 if:
- Low perceived value and low uncertainty incidence.

---

## H5 (Admin): publish with edits

- "After publishing, I still need to edit dates or text without fully unpublishing."
- "Hard lock after publish increases my operational risk." 

Policy preference:
- "Preferred model"
  - Hard lock
  - Controlled edits with audit log
  - Fully editable live

Confirm H5 if:
- Controlled edits chosen by majority and hard lock is minority choice.

Reject H5 if:
- Hard lock selected by majority.

---

## H6 (Admin): late team management

- "I usually invite judges/team members close to judging start."

Behavioral check:
- "Typical invite timing"
  - >2 weeks before judging
  - 1-2 weeks before
  - 3-7 days before
  - 1-2 days before
  - Same day

Confirm H6 if:
- >=50% indicate invites 1-7 days before judging, with concentration near 1-2 days.

Reject H6 if:
- Most invites happen >2 weeks prior.
